## Writing Guidance
<div>
  <h3>Keep Buttons Concise</h3>
  <p>Limit buttons to three words or less. This keeps buttons scannable and easy to understand. Limiting the length of buttons also ensures that we establish context through surrounding elements on the page.</p>
  <div class='exampleImages'>
    <div class='wrapper -do'>
      <p class='-do'>Do</p>
      <figure>
        <img src='/img/Content_Guidance_Images/Buttons/DO_Buttons_Concise.png' alt='Button with concise text "Submit".' />
        <figcaption></figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>

    <div class='wrapper -dont'>
      <p class='-dont'>Don't</p>
      <figure>
        <img src='/img/Content_Guidance_Images/Buttons/DONT_Buttons_Concise.png' alt='Button with overly detailed text "Click Here to Submit Your Information".' />
        <figcaption></figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>
  </div>

  <h3>Use Title Case</h3>
  <p>Use title case by capitalizing the first letter of major words, like nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs. Keep short conjunctions, articles, and prepositions lowercase. This style highlights button text, aiding user scanning.</p>
  <div class='exampleImages'>
    <div class='wrapper -do'>
      <p class='-do'>Do</p>
      <figure>
        <img src='/img/Content_Guidance_Images/Buttons/DO_Buttons_Capitalization.png' alt='Button labeled "Add to Cart" in title case.' />
        <figcaption></figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>

    <div class='wrapper -dont'>
      <p class='-dont'>Don't</p>
      <figure>
        <img src='/img/Content_Guidance_Images/Buttons/DONT_Buttons_Capitalization.png' alt='Three examples of a button labeled "Add to Cart" in all uppercase, lowercase, and sentence case.' />
        <figcaption></figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>
  </div>

  <h3>Be Specific in Link Text</h3>
  <p>Avoid using generic phrases like "click here" or "learn more" whenever possible.</p>
  <div class='exampleImages'>
    <div class='wrapper -do'>
      <p class='-do'>Do</p>
      <figure>
        <img src='/img/Content_Guidance_Images/Buttons/DO_Buttons_Specific.png' alt='A link showing a clear call to action "Download the User Guide".' />
        <figcaption></figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>

    <div class='wrapper -dont'>
      <p class='-dont'>Don't</p>
      <figure>
        <img src='/img/Content_Guidance_Images/Buttons/DONT_Buttons_Specific.png' alt='Screen with vague link texts: "Proceed to Information", "More Details Here", and "Click Here".' />
        <figcaption></figcaption>
      </figure>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
